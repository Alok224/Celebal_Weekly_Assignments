Hospital Emergency Care Policy

Emergency medical services are available twenty-four hours a day.

Patients with life-threatening conditions receive immediate medical attention.

Emergency treatment is provided regardless of payment status during critical situations.

Patient priority is determined according to medical urgency through triage assessment.

Emergency physicians may request laboratory tests, imaging, or specialist consultation as required.

Critical patients may be transferred directly to the Intensive Care Unit.

Family members will be informed about the patient's condition whenever possible.

Hospital security maintains safe access to emergency care facilities.

Emergency medical records are securely documented for future reference.

Patients requiring specialized treatment may be transferred to another facility if necessary.
