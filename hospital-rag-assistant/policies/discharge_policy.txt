Hospital Discharge Policy

Patients will be discharged only after receiving approval from the treating physician.

A discharge summary will be provided containing diagnosis, treatment details, medications, and follow-up instructions.

Patients should carefully review medication schedules before leaving the hospital.

Pending laboratory reports may be collected later if not available during discharge.

Hospital property must be returned before completing discharge formalities.

Patients are encouraged to schedule follow-up appointments as recommended by their physician.

Medical records remain confidential after discharge.

Discharge timing depends on completion of billing and administrative procedures.

Patients should immediately contact the hospital if complications occur after discharge.

The hospital recommends following all discharge instructions to support recovery.
