Hospital Insurance Policy

The hospital accepts insurance from approved insurance providers.

Patients must present valid insurance identification and authorization documents during admission.

Cashless treatment is available only for eligible insurance plans.

Insurance approval depends on the provider's terms and policy coverage.

Expenses not covered by insurance must be paid directly by the patient.

Pre-authorization may be required for surgeries and specialized treatments.

Insurance claims are processed after submission of all required documents.

The hospital billing department assists patients with insurance-related documentation.

Claim approval timelines are determined by the insurance provider.

Patients should verify policy coverage before receiving elective treatment.
