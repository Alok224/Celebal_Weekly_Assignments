Hospital Billing Policy

Billing charges include consultation fees, room charges, laboratory tests, medications, procedures, and other healthcare services.

A detailed billing statement will be generated before discharge.

Payments may be made through cash, debit card, credit card, UPI, or online banking.

Insurance-covered patients must submit valid insurance documents during admission.

Any charges not covered by insurance remain the responsibility of the patient.

Refunds, if applicable, will be processed within seven business days after verification.

Patients can request an itemized bill from the billing department.

Outstanding balances must be cleared before final discharge unless approved by hospital administration.

Billing disputes should be reported within thirty days of invoice generation.

The hospital maintains complete financial records for audit and compliance purposes.
