Hospital Admission Policy

All patients must provide a valid government-issued identification document during admission.

Patients should submit accurate personal details including name, age, gender, address, and contact information.

Emergency patients will be admitted immediately without prior payment. Required documentation can be completed after stabilization.

Scheduled admissions should be completed at least one hour before the planned appointment.

Patients are responsible for providing complete medical history, allergies, and current medications.

Room allocation depends on availability and the selected admission category.

Hospital staff reserve the right to verify patient identity before admission.

Advance deposits may be required for planned procedures depending on the treatment category.

Patients must sign the hospital consent form before treatment begins.

Admission records are maintained securely according to hospital privacy regulations.
